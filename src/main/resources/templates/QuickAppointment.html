Quick Appointment

This document explains how to book a quick appointment.

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Use the Book Button:

    If you are already registered, enter your email ID and password. If not, create your user account by clicking the "Create Account" link at the bottom.

  2. View the Availability of Slots:

    After logging in, navigate to the slot option, choose it, and check available slots to book your doctor.

  3. View Your Appointments:

    After booking an appointment, you can view it in the "View Appointments" option, which is available alongside the "View Slots" option.

Conclusion

This is how you can quickly book appointments with ease.
